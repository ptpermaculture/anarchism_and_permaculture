\marginpar{Anarchism and Permaculture} The outline of the part of the anarchist tradition which most interest us in this exploration surrounds that portion which works against all forms of domination, especially economic domination.  Permaculture, however variously it may have evolved, is arguably a genetic descendant of anarchist thought described by that outline.
